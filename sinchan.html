SINCHAN is an annual dance showcase of CHHANDANEERH held every year in the last week of December at Midnapore, West Bengal. It is a two-day dance program which promotes Indian Classical Dance as well as encourages artists and students of the Institute. During this event, CHHANDANEERH felicitates one renowned dance artist devoted to any form of Dance and unveils its annual cultural magazine ‘PRAYAS’
